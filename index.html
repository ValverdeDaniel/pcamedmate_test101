About

A dynamic analytics and clinical management platform designed by operators. While most analytic platforms are designed by software developers, Medmate’s dynamic reporting capabilities complete the continuum of care from a clinical and operations viewpoint. Reports are designed to drive improvement through actionable analysis. Medmate is designed to create initiatives around quality, utilization management and industry leading coding practices.
